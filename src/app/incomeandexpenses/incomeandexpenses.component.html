Mandatory bills

Electricity bills, water bills, telephone bills and so on. If the billing interval is less than a month, then change the amount to the monthly cost by dividing the normal invoice amount by the billing interval, ie if the water bill is billed every 6 months, divide the expected amount by 6.

Insurances

If the billing interval is less than a month, divide the amount by the billing interval, that is, for example, once a year for billable insurance with 12.

{{ budget.insurance }}€

Monthly payment programs and services

For example, Spotify, Netflix and so on. If you pay for a service less than once a month, divide the amount by the billing interval, that is, for example, for an annual fee program of 12.

{{ budget.netflix }}€

Hobby expenses per month

For example, the price of a gym card per budget. If certain types of hobby expenses occur less than once a month, then divide the amount by the monthly interval, ie for the one-time hobby fee and divide the amount by 12.

{{ budget.hobby }}€

Other expenses per month

Add those expenses to this section you can't place anywhere else. For example, toiletries, tobacco products and so on. If certain expenses are incurred less than once a month, divide the amount by the monthly interval, ie if you pay for a program once a year, then divide the amount by 12.

{{ budget.other }}€
